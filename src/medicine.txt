## 2.2.1.1. Stage 0 (TisN0M0), ductal cancer in situ
To prevent the development of second tumors in the contralateral and resected mammary gland (in the case of organ-preserving surgery) with positive RE in intracurrent (in situ) cancer, it is recommended to consider the appointment of HT using one of the following modes:
- tamoxifen 20 mg orally daily for 5 years in pre- and postmenopausal patients;
- tamoxifen 5 mg orally daily or 10 mg orally every other day for 3 years in pre- and postmenopausal patients with poor tolerance of the standard dose of tamoxifen (this is an indication not registered in the Russian Federation);
- aromatase inhibitors (letrozole 2.5 mg or anastrozole 1 mg or exemestane 25 mg orally daily) for 5 years in patients with persistent menopause.
